Absinthe AR Experience Poster

Absinthe is an immersive music festival where audiences can escape the hustle and bustle of reality into a dreamy and magical world. This festival features R&B and hip-hop music paired with live performances, interactive experience and installations.

The goal of the project is to explore the potential of combining Augmented Reality and Graphic Design to facilitate more effective communication with the audience.
